How Bookable is your business?

Bookable gets your business on track

Product

Your business has two products it needs to take care of:

  • the business itself
  • the product it creates

If you don't care for your business, it won't continue to exist. Likewise, if you don't carefully devlop a product, sales won't be made. To ensure your success, you need to focus on three things to begin with:

  • creating a solid business plan
  • creating a marketable product
  • creating a profitable pricing structure

Pitch

Many business owners believe advertising and software will automatically bring in new clients. Yet they don't spend the time learning what their audience wants to hear. Consequently, a lot of time and money is wasted with little to show.

To maximize your marketing efforts you need to accomplish the following things:

  • determine what your company stands for and how it wants to be seen
  • determine your target market & analyze your audience
  • develop a marketing plan & messaging that resonates with market

Process

How smoothly you're able to deliver your product or service determines how well your brand is received and how difficult your business day is.

Your business needs to focus on the following processes to create a great client experience and make the business thrive:

  • product production & delivery
  • the sales process
  • the administrative process
  • the customer relations process
